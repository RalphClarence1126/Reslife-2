Development of Web-Based Registration Platform for Online Enrollment

In today's pandemic environment, the Internet has become an essential tool for online platforms to emerge, and many people utilize the internet in their everyday lives, particularly at work and school. The Internet is a valuable resource for Îstudents, who depend on it on a regular basis to complete their tasks. Web-based platforms are run because of the internet. Web-based platforms make it easier for people to utilize them, and it is much easier to maintain effective communication between the sender and the recipient.

Developing a great web-based registration platform for enrollment is a challenging process. Since the project is all about developing a website that benefits and assists students in processing their enrollment online with assurance, by performing online school registration through online registration. This kind of platform will be a huge help, as it is in today's situation, so it will be in the future, especially for students in terms of taking a school admission form without going to the university.

Research Concept Contents

Research Topic

A software development project that benefits both students and teachers in performing online school registration through an online registration.

Research Key Questions

  • Do students have sufficient credentials to complete the school registration?
  • What are the emerging issues and concerns in the implementation of Student Information System?
  • What will be the developers’ response to the concerns and issues?
  • Can this also be accessible not only for the student, but also for the school officials?

Contribution of the Research to the discipline

It will become easier for students and teachers to manage their enrollment records. This study will also be available to future researchers who are studying relevant lessons about this kind of topic.
